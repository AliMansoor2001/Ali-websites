    • The Author

    JEFF KINNEY

    Style: Art, Humor, Young Adult Fiction

    Jeff Kinney is an American author and cartoonist best known for his popular children's book series, "Diary of a Wimpy Kid." The series follows the misadventures of a middle school student named Greg Heffley through his diary entries and doodles. The books are known for their humorous and relatable storytelling, capturing the challenges and experiences of growing up. Jeff Kinney's unique combination of text and illustrations has made the "Diary of a Wimpy Kid" series a favorite among young readers around the world. In addition to the main series, Kinney has also written and illustrated several spin-off books, including "Diary of an Awesome Friendly Kid" and "Rowley Jefferson's Awesome Friendly Adventure." His books have been adapted into successful movies and have garnered numerous awards and accolades.
